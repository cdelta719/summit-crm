### Priority Outreach Order
1. **Real estate & property management** — Fastest sales cycle, clearest ROI, most prospects
2. **Dental/medical practices** — Urgent pain (missed calls), quantifiable ROI
3. **Law firms** — Highest deal size potential (Enterprise tier), clear billable hour math
4. **Marketing agencies** — Fast decision makers, tech-savvy, quick to adopt
5. **Financial advisors** — High LTV clients, compliance needs push to Enterprise tier
6. **Construction** — Less competition, underserved by AI vendors
7. **B2B sales** — May already have tools, but ClawStart fills gaps

### Weekly Outreach Targets

1. **Not a chatbot** — ClawStart takes action, not just answers questions

3. **Integrates with existing tools** — Doesn't replace, enhances
4. **2-week deployment** — Fast time to value
5. **Colorado-based** — Local service, local understanding
