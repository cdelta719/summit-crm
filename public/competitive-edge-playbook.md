# STRATEGY 1: THE "COLLISON INSTALL"

## The Origin Story

When Stripe was starting out, Patrick and John Collison did something nobody else did. Instead of saying "check out our product and sign up when you're ready," they'd say **"give me your laptop"** and install Stripe for the person right there, on the spot. No friction. No "I'll think about it." By the end of the conversation, they were already a customer.

This is the most powerful sales move in existence for a service like ClawStart.

## How ClawStart Does the Collison Install

**The Move:** During a demo call or in-person meeting, don't just SHOW them what the AI can do. **SET IT UP FOR THEM. LIVE. RIGHT NOW.**

### The Script:

*"[Name], I can tell you're interested but you're not sure if it'll actually work for your business. Totally fair. Here's what I want to do — give me 15 minutes right now. I'm going to set up a basic version of your AI assistant while we're on this call. You'll see it working before we hang up. If you love it, we'll talk about the full deployment. If not, you've lost nothing. Deal?"*

### What You Set Up Live (15-20 min):
1. A basic email triage bot connected to their inbox (or a test inbox)
2. An auto-responder for their most common inquiry type
3. A scheduling link with AI confirmation messages
4. A simple lead follow-up sequence

### Why This Works:
- **Eliminates "I need to think about it"** — they're already using it
- **Eliminates "does it actually work?"** — they just saw it work
- **Eliminates "is it hard to set up?"** — you did it in 15 minutes
- **Creates ownership** — psychologically, they now OWN this thing. Taking it away feels like loss.
- **Massive differentiation** — no competitor does this. Nobody.

### Implementation:
- Have a rapid-deploy template for each vertical (pre-built, just needs customization)
- Practice the 15-minute install until you can do it blindfolded
- Always have your setup tools ready on every call — laptop open, templates loaded
- Even if they don't close that day, they'll have a working bot reminding them of what they're missing

### The Follow-Up After a Collison Install:

Day 1: *"Hey [Name], your AI has already [handled X emails / booked Y appointments / responded to Z leads] since we set it up yesterday. Here's a quick summary. Ready to go full deployment?"*

This is a layup. They've already tasted it. Now they need the full meal.

# STRATEGY 2: THE FREE TRIAL / PILOT PROGRAM

## Structure: 7-Day Free Pilot

**Who qualifies:** Prospects who scored 60+ on the BANT scorecard but need a push to commit.

**What they get:** A basic bot deployment (1-2 workflows) running for 7 days, free.

**What you get:** A nearly guaranteed close at the end of 7 days.

### The Pitch:

*"Tell you what — I'm confident enough in this that I'll put my money where my mouth is. Let me set up a 7-day pilot for [Company Name]. Basic version — email triage and lead response. You use it for a week, see the results, and then we decide together if it makes sense to go full. If it doesn't work, you walk away and I eat the setup time. Fair?"*

### Pilot Rules:
1. **Only for qualified prospects** — don't give free pilots to tire-kickers
2. **Limited scope** — 1-2 workflows only. Enough to prove value, not enough to feel complete.
3. **Daily check-ins** — Email them every day with a summary of what the bot did
4. **Metrics tracking** — Track everything: emails handled, leads responded to, time saved
5. **Day 5 call** — Schedule the closing conversation for Day 5 (not Day 7). Build in urgency.
6. **Clear transition** — "The pilot showed [specific results]. The full deployment does 10x this. Ready to go?"

### The Conversion Path:
```
Free Pilot (7 days) → Full Setup ($5K-$15K) → Managed Care ($250-$750/mo)
```

### Why This Works:
- **Loss aversion** — After 7 days of having it, losing it hurts more than the price
- **Proof over promises** — They've seen real results with their own data
- **Reduces risk** — They're not buying blind
- **Data for the close** — "Your bot handled 47 emails and responded to 12 leads this week. Without it, that's 8 hours of work and 3 missed leads. The full deployment is 5x this."

### Guard Rails:
- Max 2 active pilots at a time (you can't give away all your time)
- Prospect must sign a pilot agreement (commits to a decision meeting on Day 5-7)
- Track pilot conversion rate — should be 70%+

# STRATEGY 9: PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

## Why $5,000 Is the Right Number

- **$3,000 feels cheap** — Triggers "is this legit?" Cheap = low value perception.
- **$4,999 feels gimmicky** — Round numbers feel confident and premium.
- **$5,000 feels serious but accessible** — It's a real investment, but any profitable business can do it.
- **$10,000+ is Enterprise territory** — Save that for the big tier.

$5,000 is the "Goldilocks price" for small-to-mid businesses. It's enough to be taken seriously, but not enough to require board approval.

## The Anchoring Play

**ALWAYS present Enterprise first.**

*"We have two plans. Enterprise is $15,000 setup with $750/month — that's for larger organizations that need multi-agent deployment, advanced integrations, and premium support. Then we have Growth at $5,000 setup and $250/month — perfect for most businesses getting started. Based on what you've told me, I'd recommend Growth to start, with the option to upgrade as you scale."*

**What just happened:**
- You said "$15,000" first. That's the anchor.
- $5,000 now feels like a DEAL compared to $15,000.
- You recommended the lower tier — you're not upselling, you're advising.
- "Option to upgrade" plants the seed for future expansion.

## Making $250/Month Feel Like Nothing

**The Daily Breakdown:**
*"$250/month works out to about $8 per day. That's less than your morning Starbucks run. Except this coffee answers your emails, books your appointments, follows up with your leads, and works weekends."*

**The Employee Comparison:**
*"$250/month is what you'd pay a part-time admin for about 6 hours of work. Except this works 24/7/365 — that's 720 hours per month. You're paying about 35 cents per hour."*

**The Revenue Frame:**
*"$250/month. You told me you close about $[X] per [deal/patient/client]. One additional [deal] every other month more than pays for the entire year. Everything after that is pure profit."*

## Payment Plans

For businesses that want to start but cash flow is tight:

**Standard Payment Plan:**
- $2,500 at signing
- $2,500 at 30 days (before the system even goes fully live)

**Script:**
*"We offer a payment plan — $2,500 to get started, and $2,500 at the 30-day mark. By the time the second payment hits, you'll already be seeing results."*

**Why it works:**
- Cuts the initial barrier in half
- By day 30, they've seen the value — second payment feels justified
- No interest, no complexity — just two payments
